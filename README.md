## About Me
My name is Alejandro Ortiz Fuentes, also known on the internet as Aleorfu. From a young age, I’ve been passionate about game development. At the age of 10, I started creating games using [Scratch](https://scratch.mit.edu/), and it wasn’t long before I moved on to [Unity](https://unity.com/). While I didn’t publish any games, my goal was never about publishing. Instead, I was driven by a desire to learn how games were made.

This journey led me to pursue [advanced vocational training](https://sevilla-reyes.safa.edu/) in Multi-Platform Application Development. Now that I’ve completed my studies, I am focused on mastering the C# language. Its versatility and compatibility with many platforms enable me to create a wide range of applications, from web and desktop applications to video games in [Godot](https://godotengine.org/), which is open-source and gives me more freedom for my creations. **Developing video games remains my passion**.

In late 2024, while job hunting, I realized the importance of having a portfolio. Therefore, I am starting to develop small projects to continue learning and showcase my skills.

Soon, I plan to develop a website to centralize my online profile.
